Brief Introduction

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. The game features a fantasy world called Teyvat, which is divided into several regions. Players control a character known as the Traveler, who embarks on a journey to search for their lost sibling and unravel the mysteries of Teyvat.

Explore the vast world, encounter various characters, engage in battles, and uncover the secrets of this enchanting universe!
